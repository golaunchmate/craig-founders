Founder Agent Onboarding

1/5

The Problem

Getting Founders to Agents Was Manual

When a new founder joins, they need an agent. But setting that up—creating the agent, loading relevant context, matching it to the founder's situation—was all manual work.

Every new founder meant someone had to configure things by hand. That doesn't scale.

2/5

The Build

Automatched Pre-Trained Agents

Michael built a new onboarding experience that automatically pairs founders with agents.

The key insight: agents can be pre-trained with relevant context before they ever meet the founder. Industry knowledge, common patterns, useful frameworks—all loaded and ready.

When a founder shows up, the system matches them to an agent that already has the right background.

3/5

Why It Matters

From Cold Start to Running Start

Before: Founders got blank-slate agents. Weeks of context-building before they were useful.

Now: Founders get agents with relevant context from day one. The agent already understands their space.

This is the difference between "tell me about your business" and "I see you're in B2B SaaS—let's talk about your GTM."

4/5

Current Status

Growth Agents, Streamlined

The system is built to support growth agents—agents specifically designed to help founders scale.

The onboarding flow is now streamlined: founder signs up, gets matched, starts working with a contextually-aware agent immediately.

Less friction, faster time-to-value.

5/5
