From Dual-Track to Focused Execution

December 2025 // scroll to read

Starting Point

Running two tracks at once

Started the week trying to validate two channels simultaneously: QR codes and Instagram.

The thinking was to test both and see which one got traction. But splitting focus meant neither was getting full attention.

1/5

The Signal

Instagram is working

Then the data came in:

300+
views on Instagram

That's real traction. People are actually watching. Decision: go Instagram-only. Drop the QR track and focus where there's signal.

2/5

The Realization

I'm being passive

But then a harder realization hit: I don't actually have anything to offer yet.

No restaurant partnerships, no deals to promote. I'm building an audience but I'm just... posting content. Passive.

Views are great, but views without a deal to offer is just entertainment, not a business.

3/5

The Pivot

Fake door test

Smart solution: run a fake door test.

Flip the chicken-and-egg problem.

4/5

Where I Am Now

Winter break goal: first restaurant

  • Pivoted from dual-track to Instagram-only
  • 300+ views proving the channel works
  • Fake door test to capture demand
  • Use signups as leverage for restaurant partnerships

Winter break mission: land the first restaurant partner.
