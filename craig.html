Architecture Evolution

MemCore + Borderline

Architecture thinking evolved significantly. Now framing LaunchMate as:

MEMCORE
The main agent that knows everything about you. Holds all context.
BORDERLINE
A board of directors of specialized agents—friction mapping, pre-meeting narratives, transcription processing.

Specialists stay lean, MemCore holds context, humans stay in the loop for specialist access.
