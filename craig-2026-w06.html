The Hypothesis

Would "the agent that knows you" work at scale?

Going into Boot Camp, I had a bet: founders would resonate with agents that already knew them.

But 80 founders in 5 hours? That's a stress test.

The approach worked — brilliantly. But the real validation came from somewhere unexpected.

2/5

The Moment

Zach was scared of losing his agent

Zach Brenner, an In-Flight founder, pulled me aside. He was worried that provisioning a new agent would replace the one we'd built together.

That fear = attachment.

That's not "useful tool" behavior. That's proof founders feel known.

"Fear of losing your agent" is the signal I didn't know I was looking for.
